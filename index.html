Projects

EventScene

EventScene is a React Native app deployable to IOS and Android Platforms. EventScene helps tourists and locals alike track events/venues in their immediate area through the use of Google Maps, thus being more visually friendly and interactive than most other event apps. EventScene tracks the users location and constantly updates their coordinates as they drag their finger across the map. A simple tap of the screen will enable multiple markers within a five mile radius to pop up on the map with details of each event/venue. More advanced features include filtering different types of events, saving a URL to the users profile, opening a URL for tickets and additional info, and embedding the users uber account into the app to catch a ride to the next event in their area.
