About Me

Hi, I'm Alexander and I'm a Full Stack Software Developer with a strong passion for creating Web and Mobile Apps. Before I became a developer, I was a Sales and Operations Manager in the retail industry. I've always had a knack for solving problems and taking on big challenges. This, along with my innovative creativity led me to explore software development as a career and lifestyle.

I discovered DigitalCrafts Coding BootCamp located in Atlanta Tech Village and was accepted into their November 2017 Cohort. The knowlegde I've gained from DigitalCrafts has become invaluable to me as a developer. I've been able focus my time on sharpening the skills I've learned during the program as well as learn new technoliges to stay current in the ever growing tech industry.

EventScene is an interactive, user-friendly React Native app that tracks a users location and utilizes Google Maps to pin point marker positions of events and venues within a set radius. Advanced features include filtering different types of events, saving a URL to the users profile, opening a URL for tickets and additional info, and embedding the users uber account into the app to catch a ride to the next event in their area.
